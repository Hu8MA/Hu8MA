### About

I'm a Research Engineer at **Era Vision**, working at the intersection of software engineering and applied AI research. My work is guided by a single ambition: **building lightweight, fast, and simple models** for the fields I care about — without compromising on rigor or elegance.

I started as a programmer, grew into a developer, then a software engineer, and now I architect systems and design neural networks. Languages, frameworks, and platforms are tools — what matters is the model, the architecture, the idea behind them. I read deeply into mathematics and the philosophy of software development because I believe **simplicity is earned, not assumed**.
